About ENABLE

Building futures
through STEM.

Our story

Founded in Durham, with global ambition.

ENABLE aims to create significant impact with the support of our dedicated members and volunteers, in both our local and global community.

Our work is centered on an unwavering commitment to expanding STEM education and project funding for local underprivileged youth — through sponsorship, service, and a genuine desire to create social change.

What we do

Our initiatives

01

Inspiring youth education

We empower a generation of underrepresented and low-income youth to reach their full potential through higher education and extracurricular opportunities. Education builds not just a career, but a community.

02

Project sponsorship

We resource student projects and ideas to empower young thinkers. Imagination shouldn't be limited by social or economic factors when given the funding and support to be developed.

03

Helping communities

We build communities, especially in overlooked areas and neighborhoods — through fairs, giveaways, and engagement with local communities and city governments.

Why we exist

Empowering communities

ENABLE was founded by four individuals of vastly different backgrounds, some of whom grew up in poor and overlooked neighborhoods. We understand the struggles of being raised in low-income and underrepresented communities — particularly in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Just like those who supported us, we believe that through personal guidance and empathy, anyone can develop their passion in STEM.

We believe in the potential of every individual. Since our founding, we have been guided by support, empowerment, and progress. Our mission is to inspire action and make a tangible difference in underdeveloped communities through expanding STEM education and funding.
