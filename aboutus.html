About Us

Our Story

Established in Durham, North Carolina, ENABLE aims to create significant impact with the support of our dedicated members and volunteers in the local and global community. Our work is centered around the unwavering passion and commitment to expanding STEM education and project funding to local underprivileged youths through sponsorships, service, and a desire to create social change.

OUR INITIATIVES

Inspiring Youth Education

At ENABLE, we empower a generation of underrepresented and low-income minorities to reach their full potential by pursuing higher education and extracurricular opportunities. In an ever-changing world, education is not only important for building a career, but also a community.

Project Sponsorship

ENABLE's commitment to sponsoring and resourcing student projects and ideas aims to empower youth thinkers. We believe that imagination and ideas of youth should not be limited by social or economic factors when given the opportunity, funding, and support to be developed.

Helping Communities

The overarching aim of ENABLE centers around building communities, especially amongst those in overlooked areas and neighborhoods. We participate in a variety of community events, fairs, giveaways, and other forms of engagement with local communities or city governments.

Empowering Communities

ENABLE was founded by four individuals of vastly different and unique backgrounds, some of whom who grew up in poor and overlooked neighborhoods. We understand the struggles that come with being raised in low-income and underrepresented communities, particularly in STEM (sciences, technology, engineering, and mathematics). Just like those us who supported us and our overlooked communities in the past, we believe that through personal guidance and empathy, anyone can develop their passion in STEM.

At ENABLE, we believe in the potential of every individual. Since our establishment, we have been guided by the principles of support, empowerment, and progress. Our mission is to inspire action and make a tangible difference in underdeveloped communities through expanding STEM education and funding.
