For teachers & students

A nonprofit built around young people's ideas.

At ENABLE, our role isn't only to promote higher education to underrepresented children — it's to give constant support and guidance, and to fund personalized STEM projects and initiatives.

We let students use their imagination to develop their own ideas, then pair them with mentorship from our founders and network to advance the interests of their communities.
