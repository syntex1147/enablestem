FOR TEACHERS & STUDENTS

Welcome to Our Nonprofit

At ENABLE, our role is not only to promote higher education to underrepresented children, but to also give constant support and guidance to funding personalized STEM projects and initiatives. We allow children to use their imagination to come up with personalized ideas, and through STEM mentorship from our founders and connections, assist them in developing unique STEM projects to advance the interests of their communities
